Uses and Career Options

Learning photography opens up exciting opportunities both as a hobby and as a career. Photography is widely used in media, advertising, journalism, art, and personal storytelling.

Career Options:

  • Professional Photographer (weddings, events, portraits)
  • Photojournalist (news and media)
  • Wildlife and Nature Photographer
  • Fashion Photographer
  • Commercial & Advertising Photographer
  • Freelance Photographer
