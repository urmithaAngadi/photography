Essential Tools to Learn Photography

  • Camera: DSLR or Mirrorless cameras are most commonly used.
  • Lenses: Different lenses (wide-angle, macro, telephoto) help in capturing various perspectives.
  • Tripod: For stability and sharp images.
  • Lighting: Natural light, flash, and studio lights.
  • Editing Software: Tools like Adobe Lightroom and Photoshop for post-processing.
